# Partners
We're alway looking for partnering up with people and businesses interested in exploring possibilities and opportunities in the future of EEG-based applications.

Here is a listing of our current partnerships:

## BrainBit (EEG Hardware & Software)
BrainBit is an R&D-driven neurotechnology company with headquarters in California, an office in Poland, and manufacturing in China. 

We combine advanced hardware, intelligent software, and developer-friendly tools to power neurofeedback providers, wellness brands, researchers, and IT-based neuro businesses worldwide.

Our scalable platform transforms EEG, EMG, and ECG signals into adaptive, data-driven experiences and supports an expanding wearable ecosystem from headsets to sensing belts. Designed for cross-species innovation, BrainBit enables pioneers to build the future of brain-and-body technology for humans and animals alike.

Contact: Boris Goldstein.
