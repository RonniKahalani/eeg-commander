# Code of Conduct
To assure a non-toxic, no-a**hole culture, any person aspirering to join the team has to understand our values and red lines.

## Our Standards
**Expected Behavior:**
- Be respectful, kind, and inclusive
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration
- Be open to different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Celebrate creativity and experimentation with brain-computer interfaces
- Help newcomers feel welcome

**Unacceptable Behavior:**
- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others’ private information (doxxing)
- Any form of discriminatory behavior
- Spamming, self-promotion without permission, or excessive off-topic content
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or community setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
- Ban temporarily or permanently any member for inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to **all spaces** managed by this project, including:
- GitHub (Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions)
- Discord server
- Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and other official social channels
- Any official events or meetings

## Reporting Guidelines
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the project maintainers at:

**Email:** [admin@eeg-commander.com]  
**Discord:** DM to @CommunityAdmin (or designated moderators)

All reports will be handled with **confidentiality** and will be reviewed fairly and promptly.
